Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, delicious, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g pancetta, diced
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente.
  2. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated pecorino cheese.
  3. In a pan, fry the pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the pancetta. Toss to combine.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and quickly add the egg and cheese mixture, stirring vigorously to create a creamy sauce. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
