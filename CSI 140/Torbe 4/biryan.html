Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful Indian rice dish made with marinated chicken, aromatic spices, and basmati rice. It's a perfect blend of taste and aroma.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Salt
  • Ghee or oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee or oil and fry the onions until golden brown. Remove and set
