Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss to coat in the garlic oil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Add grated Parmesan cheese and toss again.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve.

Doro Wat

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp berbere spice mix
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled

Instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onions and cook until soft.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the berbere spice mix and cook, stirring, for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Add the hard-boiled eggs and cook for a few more minutes.
  7. Serve hot with injera or rice.

Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat. Grease with butter or oil.
  5. Pour batter onto the griddle and cook until bubbles form on the surface. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, berries, or whipped cream.
